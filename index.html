OUR STORY

Bramble is a small, contemporary pet bed and accessories brand based in England.

Founded in Devon by Ali, her vision was to create a beautiful, eco-friendly and high quality pet bed. Using her knowledge of fabrics and construction learnt whilst studying Fashion Design at Kingston University, she came up with our signature wool filled bed.

All products are designed and made in England by Ali, using only organic fabrics and pure British wool.
